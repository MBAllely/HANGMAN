Slow & Painful Death Simulator

This is a pretty dark game. It's pretty whack. So here's the deal:

You gotta save this unfortunate schmuck from death. First, you guess a letter. If the mystery word contains it, congratulations! Mr. Schmuck passes a death save. But if you guess a letter that isn't contained: bad news. We'll add a limb to the death count. You get five tries. Mr. Schmuck has three child support payments to make every month. Without him , his children starve. And probably die. No pressure
